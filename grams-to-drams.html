About Weight Units

The gram (g) is a metric unit of weight defined as one thousandth of a kilogram. It's widely used in scientific contexts and in most countries worldwide.

The dram (dr) is a unit of mass in the avoirdupois system, equal to 1/16 of an ounce or 1.7718452 grams. It has historical use in pharmacy and is still used in some contexts today.
