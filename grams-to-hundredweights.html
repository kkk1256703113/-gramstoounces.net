About Weight Units

The gram (g) is a metric unit of weight defined as one thousandth of a kilogram. It's widely used in scientific contexts and in most countries worldwide.

The hundredweight (cwt) is an imperial unit of weight equal to 112 pounds in the UK (long hundredweight) or 100 pounds in the US (short hundredweight). This converter uses the UK long hundredweight, which equals approximately 50.8 kilograms.
